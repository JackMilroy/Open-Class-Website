Mayfield Engineering Class

The class officially titled "Exploration of Engineering" in the Mayfield course handbook is constantly under development. This website will serve as a hub to see where all of this content comes together for each of the different students that are enrolled in the course. Things will likely change year-to-year, and each individual student's journey will be unique. Here we can share that work for all to see and to maintain for future learners.
